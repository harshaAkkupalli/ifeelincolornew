## Meet the Four Roles

IFEELINCOLOR is built on a simple idea: different people need different doors into the same beautiful house. Here are the four doors:

| Role | Who They Are | What They Do |
|---|---|---|
| 🎨 **Patient** | The person feeling, healing, growing. | Checks in daily, paints feelings with color, follows a personalized care plan. |
| 🩺 **Clinician** | The therapist, doctor, or counselor. | Watches over patients, sends recommendations, builds AI-powered care plans. |
| 🏢 **Organization** | A clinic, school, or care network. | Oversees a whole team of clinicians and groups of patients. |
| 👑 **Super Admin** | The platform owner (you). | Runs everything: pricing, content, users, emails, branding. |

# Role 1 — The Patient (Your End User)

### Role Overview
**The Patient is the heart of IFEELINCOLOR — someone who wants to understand their feelings, track how their body responds, and follow a gentle, personalized wellness plan.**

---

### 🌅 Step-by-Step Workflow — A Day in the Life of a Patient

#### **Phase A — Getting In**

1. **Open the app** (either on a phone browser or the IFEELINCOLOR mobile app).
2. **Land on the Welcome Screen.** Tap the big pink button labeled **"Open the app to feel better."**
3. **Choose your door.** Tap the **"Patient"** toggle pill at the top.
4. **Sign in or sign up.**
   - *New here?* Tap the **"Sign Up"** tab, type your name, email, and a password, then tap **"Create Account."**
   - *Returning?* Tap **"Sign In,"** type your email and password, then tap **"Log In."**
   - *Forgot password?* Tap the small **"Forgot password?"** link — type your email — check your inbox for a reset link.
5. **(Optional) Turn on Face / Fingerprint login.** The first time you sign in, the app asks if you'd like to log in faster next time using your fingerprint or face. Tap **"Yes, set it up."**

> [3D Animation Concept: A warm, smiling 3D character standing in front of a glowing pink doorway shaped like a heart. As they tap their finger to a soft fingerprint scanner floating mid-air, a stream of pastel rainbow colors swirls around them and gently lifts them through the door.]

---

#### **Phase B — The Welcome Home Screen**

6. **You arrive at your Home Screen.** You see a friendly greeting ("Hi Luna 👋"), the date, a glowing **"Daily Check-In"** tile, and four quick-action squares: *Daily Check-In · Recommendations · Assessment · 3D History.*
7. **Notice the top of the screen.** There's a colorful logo (tap it any time to come back home), a bell icon (your notifications), and a red **SOS** button (always available — tap it any time you need urgent help).
8. **Welcome popup (first visit of the day).** A soft, animated card may appear welcoming you. Read it, then tap **"Let's begin."** (It only shows once per day.)

> [3D Animation Concept: A 3D character stands inside a soft, sunlit room. Floating glass panels gently slide down from above showing the day's weather, a colorful "Daily Check-In" sphere pulsing in the center, and friendly icons like a bell and a heart hovering at eye level.]

---

#### **Phase C — The Daily Check-In (The 9-Step Magic Moment)**

This is the soul of the app. It takes 2–3 minutes.

9. **Tap the big "Daily Check-In" tile** in the center of your home screen. The screen smoothly opens into a full-screen, calm, indigo-night experience.

10. **Step 1 — The Body Map.** You see a soft 3D outline of a person with 12 glowing dots placed on different body parts (Head, Chest, Stomach, Throat, Legs, etc.). The screen says: *"Tap where you are feeling something in your body."* **Tap one or more dots** (they light up in color when selected).

> [3D Animation Concept: A holographic, gender-neutral 3D human figure rotates gently in space. As the user taps each glowing dot (head, chest, stomach), a pulsing ring of warm color radiates outward from that body part with a soft chime.]

11. **Step 2 — How Strong Is It?** A friendly question appears: *"How strong is this feeling in your body?"* **Drag the slider** from 0 (barely there) to 10 (very strong).

12. **Step 3 — The Sensation.** Pick a word that describes the feeling: *"warm," "tight," "buzzing," "heavy,"* etc. **Tap the chip** that matches. Some questions specific to that body part may also appear — answer them by tapping Yes/No or typing.

13. **Step 4 — The Feelings Wheel.** The app suggests one or two feelings that match what you just selected (e.g., "calm," "anxious," "joyful"). **Tap the feeling** that fits best, or tap **"Explore the full Feelings Wheel"** to see more.

> [3D Animation Concept: A beautifully colored 3D wheel (yellow, blue, red, green, orange, purple, grey segments) slowly spins in 3D space. As the user hovers over a slice, that color floats forward, and tiny emotion words orbit around it like satellites.]

14. **Step 5 — Reflection.** A short sentence like *"Right now, I feel a tight feeling of anxious in my chest, and it shows up as red."* You can edit the sentence or just tap **"Continue."**

15. **Step 6 — A Calming Activity.** The app gives you a **60-second regulation step** — a breathing orb to follow, a stretch, or a grounding exercise. The screen shows: *"Try this step for 60 seconds."*

> [3D Animation Concept: A glowing 3D orb floats gently up and down with the user's breath. As the orb expands, soft pastel light fills the screen; as it contracts, the colors gently retreat. The 3D character sits cross-legged in front, breathing in sync.]

16. **Step 7 — How Did That Feel?** Four buttons appear:
    - **"I did it"** ✅
    - **"I need another step"** 🔄
    - **"I want to choose a different feeling"** ↩️
    - **"I want to pause"** ⏸️
    Tap whichever feels right.

17. **Step 8 — Color Shift.** The app asks: *"After doing the step, what color are you ending with? What feeling? How strong is it now?"* **Pick a new color, a new feeling, and slide the new intensity number.**

18. **Step 9 — Your Color Journey Summary.** A beautiful summary card appears: *"You started feeling Red (8/10) and ended feeling Green (4/10). That's a wonderful shift."* **Tap "Done"** to save your check-in forever.

> [3D Animation Concept: Two glowing 3D color spheres (a fiery red and a calm green) float side by side. A shimmering arrow of mixed pastels arcs between them, and a small "−4" intensity badge twinkles in the middle. Confetti made of soft color petals gently falls behind.]

---

#### **Phase D — Reviewing Your Journey**

19. **Tap the "3D History" tile** on your home screen (or the "Journey" tab at the bottom). You'll see your **Completed Assessments** panel showing your progress (e.g., 3 of 3 done) and a list of every check-in you've ever completed.

20. **Tap any past assessment card.** A full-screen panel slides up showing:
    - The exact date and time.
    - A severity badge (low / moderate / high / critical).
    - A mini body map showing what you felt that day.
    - The **Color Shift** (start color → end color).
    - The **AI Wellness Plan** with bullet points and an encouraging note.
    - All recommendations that were sent to you that day.

> [3D Animation Concept: A 3D filing cabinet floats in space, drawers opening and closing in slow motion. Each drawer pulls out a glowing card — a tiny holographic body map, a color-shift arrow, and a stack of recommendation cards — all hovering for the user to read.]

21. **Want a printed copy?** Tap the **"Download PDF"** button at the top. A small menu pops up:
    - **Standard PDF (Recommended)** — a clean, branded report perfect for sharing with your doctor.
    - **Animated PDF** — a fancier version with body-map snapshots (best on a desktop or laptop).
    Tap your choice. The file downloads and you can email it, print it, or save it.

---

#### **Phase E — Following Your Care Plan**

22. **Tap the "Recommendations" tile** on your home screen. You see a tabbed list: **All · From Clinician · From Organization · From IFEELINCOLOR.**
23. **Tap any recommendation card.** A detail panel opens — read the description, watch any video, follow the steps, or open any helpful link.
24. **Notifications.** Whenever your clinician (or the IFEELINCOLOR team) sends you a new recommendation, the **bell icon** at the top shows a colored dot. Tap the bell to see your full feed.

> [3D Animation Concept: A 3D character sits on a comfy floating cushion, reading a holographic notification card. As they tap "Mark as Done," a soft confetti burst of color petals fills the air and a checkmark badge pins itself to the card.]

---

#### **Phase F — Subscribing to a Clinician or a Plan**

25. **Tap the "Subscribe" link** in the bottom navigation. You see three tabs: **Portal Plan · Clinician Plan · Organization Plan.**
26. **Choose a plan.** Tap a plan card (e.g., "Pro — $19/month").
27. **Enter your card details** in the secure Stripe payment form.
28. **Tap "Subscribe Now."** A success message appears, and the plan is now active. You'll see a green banner at the top of the page showing your active plan, days remaining, and renewal date.

---

#### **Phase G — Profile, Settings & Emergencies**

29. **Tap the "Profile" tab at the bottom.** Here you can: update your name, photo, location (so we can find nearby doctors), language, and accessibility preferences.
30. **Toggle on "Calm Mode"** in Settings if you prefer softer pastel colors, larger pictograms, and read-aloud help.
31. **Need urgent help?** Tap the red **SOS button** in the top right of any screen. You'll see local crisis lines, your emergency contact, and a one-tap call button.

> [3D Animation Concept: A glowing red 3D SOS shield floats forward into focus, surrounded by a gentle pulsing aura. As the user taps it, three lifelines (phone, message, location) unfurl outward like protective wings.]

# Role 2 — The Clinician (The Care Provider)

### Role Overview
**The Clinician is the trained professional — therapist, counselor, or doctor — who watches over a group of patients, sends them recommendations, and builds personalized care plans with AI assistance.**

---

### 🩺 Step-by-Step Workflow — A Clinician's Workday

#### **Phase A — Logging In**

1. **Open the app** and tap **"Open the app to feel better"** on the welcome screen.
2. **Tap the "Clinician" toggle** at the top (it switches the screen to a warm bronze-gold theme).
3. **Sign in** with your professional email and password.
4. **(Optional) Set up biometric login** for faster access in busy clinics.

> [3D Animation Concept: A 3D clinician character in a stylish bronze-and-cream lab coat steps through a glowing teal doorway. A warm halo of golden light wraps around them as a stethoscope-shaped key floats up and unlocks a holographic clipboard.]

---

#### **Phase B — The Clinician Home Screen**

5. **Land on your Home Dashboard.** You see:
   - A friendly greeting ("Welcome back, Dr. Sarah").
   - **Four 3D stat tiles**: Total Caseload · Active Patients · Today's Alerts · Notes Logged.
   - A glowing **"AI Coach"** hero card.
   - The latest announcements from the IFEELINCOLOR team.
   - A list of recent patient alerts (e.g., "Luna logged a critical check-in 10 min ago").
6. **Notice the bottom navigation:** *Home · Patients · AI Coach · Notes · Settings.*

> [3D Animation Concept: A 3D dashboard hovers in front of the clinician — four floating crystal tiles rotate slowly showing live patient counts, while a glowing teal "AI Coach" sphere pulses invitingly in the center.]

---

#### **Phase C — Reviewing a Patient**

7. **Tap the "Patients" tab** at the bottom. A searchable list of every patient under your care appears.
8. **Type a name** in the search bar (e.g., "Luna") or scroll the list.
9. **Tap a patient row.** A full-height drawer slides in showing:
   - Their photo, name, age, and email.
   - Latest severity badge (low / moderate / high / critical).
   - Their current emotional color.
   - A **"Quick AI Assist"** mini-panel.
   - A **"3D History"** pill — tap it to see their full color journey.
   - A **"Build full AI plan"** button.

10. **Tap "3D History."** You'll see the same beautiful 3D body map and time-slider that the patient sees, plus a **Roadmap** showing every assessment, every check-in, every recommendation in chronological order.

> [3D Animation Concept: A 3D timeline ribbon stretches across the screen like a flowing river. Glowing dots dance along it — each color-coded by emotion. As the clinician scrubs the slider with a finger, the 3D body map at the top morphs in real time, showing where the patient felt each emotion.]

---

#### **Phase D — Building an AI-Powered Care Plan**

11. **Tap the "AI Coach" tab** at the bottom.
12. **Pick a patient** from the dropdown.
13. **Type your focus area** in the text box (e.g., "Help Luna with morning anxiety").
14. **Tap the glowing "Generate Plan" button.** Within seconds, the AI returns:
    - **Plan Overview** — a summary paragraph.
    - **5 Step-by-Step Recommendations** — each with rationale, suggested action, and a timeframe.
    - **Red Flags** — risks the clinician should watch for.
    - **Strengths** — patient resources to build on.
15. **Tap the checkbox next to each step** you want to send.
16. **Tap "Save selected steps to patient roadmap."** A confirmation appears, and those steps now show up in the patient's recommendation feed instantly.

> [3D Animation Concept: A 3D crystalline brain hovers above a soft glass table. As the clinician asks a question, beams of teal light pulse outward and assemble into five floating recommendation cards, each one gently dropping into a glowing roadmap river below.]

---

#### **Phase E — Sending a Custom Recommendation**

17. **Tap the "Patients" tab → pick a patient → tap "Send Recommendation."**
18. **Choose Library Mode** to pick from your pre-saved recommendation templates, **OR** **Choose Custom Mode** to build one from scratch with five content types: *Text · Video · Image · Step List · Helpful Link.*
19. **Fill in the details** (title, description, link or upload).
20. **Tap "Send to Patient."** A confirmation slides in, and the patient gets a colored notification immediately.

---

#### **Phase F — Quick Notes & Charting**

21. **Tap the "Notes" tab.** A clean composer appears.
22. **Pick the patient** from the dropdown.
23. **Type your note** (private, only visible to you and the platform).
24. **Tap "Save Note."** All past notes appear below in chronological order.

> [3D Animation Concept: A 3D leather-bound journal floats open. As the clinician types, ink-like teal letters appear and softly settle on the page. When saved, the journal closes with a warm glow and tucks itself into a floating shelf.]

---

#### **Phase G — Analytics & Reports**

25. **Tap "Analytics"** from the home Quick Actions or sidebar. You see:
    - 4 stat tiles (total check-ins, alerts, recommendations sent, active patients).
    - A **line chart** of check-in frequency over time.
    - A **pie chart** of severity distribution across all patients.
    - A **bar chart** of the most common emotions in your caseload.
    - Time-range pills: **7 days · 30 days · 90 days.**
26. **Tap any patient name** to download their **full PDF dossier** for clinical records.
27. **Tap "Export CSV"** for spreadsheet-friendly data.

---

#### **Phase H — Subscribing to a Clinician Plan & Settings**

28. **Tap "Settings"** in the bottom nav. You see your profile, plan, biometric login toggle, and Sign Out button.
29. **Tap "Subscribe"** to upgrade to **Solo · Pro · Clinic Team** tiers.
30. **Pay securely** through Stripe. Your plan activates instantly.

---

3. **Sign in** with the organization's main account credentials.

> [3D Animation Concept: A majestic 3D building made of frosted glass and warm wood tones rises out of soft clouds. Its front doors swing open as a 3D administrator character with a clipboard walks toward them, golden light spilling from inside.]

---

#### **Phase B — Organization Home**

4. **Land on the Org Dashboard.** You see:
   - The organization's name, logo, and active plan.
   - Total clinicians under your network.
   - Total patients across all clinicians.
   - Aggregated check-in volume (last 7 / 30 / 90 days).
   - A health-overview chart showing severity distribution across all patients.

> [3D Animation Concept: A 3D holographic globe filled with floating "patient" and "clinician" icons rotates slowly. Lines of soft light connect each clinician to their group of patients, visualizing the entire care network in one beautiful constellation.]

---

#### **Phase F — Custom Recommendations (Network-Wide)**

13. **Tap "Recommendations."** You can publish a recommendation that flows down to every patient in your network — for example, an organization-wide breathing exercise during exam season.
14. **Tap "Create New."** Fill in the title, body, image/video, and target audience (all patients · specific tier · specific clinicians' caseload).
15. **Tap "Publish."** Patients receive it the moment they next open the app.

---

#### **Phase G — Reports & Exports**

16. **Tap "Reports."** Generate aggregate PDF dossiers for your board meetings, monthly reviews, or compliance filings.
17. **Tap "Export CSV"** for raw data your finance or research team can analyze.

# Role 4b — The Assistant Admin (Limited-Access Helper)

### Role Overview
**The Assistant Admin is a trusted team member (e.g., a support agent or content manager) given a custom slice of admin powers — they can do exactly what the Super Admin allows, and nothing more.**

---

### 🛠️ Step-by-Step Workflow — Working as an Assistant

1. **Log in** at the same admin URL with the credentials your Super Admin emailed you.
2. **The sidebar shows only the pages you have access to** — for example, you might see *Patients · Recommendations · Email Templates* but **not** *Earnings · Plan Management.*
3. **Inside each page, your buttons are filtered too.** You might be able to *View* and *Edit* a patient, but the *Delete* button is hidden.
4. **Do your work normally.** Send recommendations, update content, draft emails — exactly like the Super Admin does, but only on your assigned pages.
5. **Tap your profile icon at the bottom of the sidebar** to update your name, photo, or password.

> [3D Animation Concept: A 3D character holding a beautifully crafted golden key with only certain teeth on it. As they walk past 12 doors in a hallway, only the doors matching their key glow open — the others stay quietly closed.]

#### How the Super Admin Sets It Up
- The Super Admin opens **"Assistant Management,"** clicks **"Add Assistant,"** and is presented with a **14-page × 5-action permission matrix.**
- They tick exactly which pages and which actions (view · create · edit · delete · send) the assistant gets.
- Quick buttons help: **"Grant All · View Only · Clear."**
- The assistant's account is created with a temp password, emailed automatically.
